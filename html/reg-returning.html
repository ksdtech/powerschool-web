Required Annual Registration for Current Students Returning in Fall 2015.

Dear Parents:

Instructions on this page are for students currently enrolled in the District. Please visit the Registration for New Students page for instructions about registering incoming students who are not currently enrolled in District schools.

Registration pages for students enrolling in Kentfield District schools in the 2014-2015 (both new students and returning students) have been revised and are now ready for your review. Please log into the PowerSchool Parent Portal and click the "Registration" icon, then review and confirm all registration pages by clicking the Submit button at the bottom of each page.

Important: Your child cannot be scheduled into classes for the coming school year until all online registation information is complete.

Please Note: If your student will not be returning to the Kentfield School District next fall, you will only need to complete Page 1 of the registration forms to indicate that a student will be leaving or graduating, and where your student will be next fall.

Registration forms are filled out based on current data you have previously provided us. You will only need to review, edit, and Submit the online pages, using the PowerSchool Parent Portal website.

If you do not have internet access or have other problems using the online registration system, please call the appropriate school office so that we can arrange for you to come in and fill out the registration forms in person; our office staff will be happy to assist you when you come in. If you have any technical difficulties completing the registration process, send an email to webmaster@kentfieldschools.org.

Confirmation of Registration for Students Currently in KSD

For each student, the information on all pages for must be reviewed, revised as necessary, and submitted by Friday, May 30, 2014, so that the school offices have time to obtain accurate enrollment counts and assign resources for the upcoming year. After completing each page, make sure to click the Submit button at the bottom of each page so that any information you update will be recorded correctly.

By reviewing and confirming the information on these pages, you will ensure that the District has the correct residential addresses, phone numbers, and email addresses, emergency contact information, and medical information in our database. The data on these pages is critical in the case of illness or emergency; please take the time to review each page and make changes as necessary.

If any of your children will not be returning to the District next fall, you still must complete Page 1 so that the school may drop the student at the end of the current school year; the District is required to report this information to the State of California, and it is also used in sending your children's records to a new school.

Portal Login Information and Lost Passwords

To begin the registration process log in at the PowerSchool Parent Portal at:

https://ksd.powerschool.com

In order to log in, you will need the User Name and Password for the Parent Access Account that you have previously created. If you have never created a Parent Access Account, please contact the school office so that they may email you the instructions for setting up your Parent Access Account. Once you have created a user name and password, please keep these in a safe place.

If you have a Parent Access Account, but have forgotten your User Name or Password, click the "Have trouble logging in?" link on the Parent Portal login page, and follow the prompts. Please note that school offices cannot access your password, so you will have to go through the steps of being issued a temporary password that you will need to change.

Reviewing and Editing Registration Forms for Each Student in Your Family

After logging in, click the button with the name of a student, and then click the Registration icon to reach the main Registration page for this student (see picture):

From this main page, follow the links to all the registration forms. As you review and edit each page, it is important to remember to click the Submit button at the bottom of each page so that your information is properly recorded.

After submitting, you will be returned to the main Registration page. A check mark will be placed next to each page that has been submitted. You may log in later to complete pages that have not been submitted, or to make corrections to pages you previously submitted.

This process must be repeated for each student in your family.

Proof of Residency

District policy requires that all families provide proof that the student resides within District boundaries. Please see our Proof of Residency page for requirements at registration time.

Immunization Records

Please note that California state law requires that parents must provide a record of required immunizations from a health provider, before students can be allowed to attend school. Please make sure that the school office has an up to date immunization record. Please see our Immunization Requirements page for more information.

Contact Information

In order for us to maintain contact with you, we also ask that you verify and update your contact information in the Parent Portal every time you change a postal or email address or phone number, not just at the annual re-registration time. At the time a change has been made, please log in to the Parent Portal at the web address shown above, and click the Contacts icon that links directly to the parent contact information page.
