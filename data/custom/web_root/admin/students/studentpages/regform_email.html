REGISTRATION INSTRUCTIONS: Before you can register your student, you must

create a Parent Access Account if you don't have an existing account, or

log in with your existing Parent Access Account and add the new student.

After you log in, please click the "Registration" icon and fill out all pages of the online regstration form as soon as possible, so that we can schedule your child's classes and provide you with additional information.

To create a parent account, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to https://ksd.powerschool.com (the PowerSchool Parent Portal).
  2. Click the "Create Account" button at the bottom of the log in page.
  3. On the Create Account page, type in your (the parent's) First Name, Last Name and Email address in the appropriate fields.
  4. Choose a simple user name (suggestion: concatenate the initial of your first name and your entire last name) and type it into the "User Name" field.
  5. Choose a password that is at least 6 characters long, and type it into the "Password" and "Re-enter Password" fields. Make sure to store your user name and password in a safe place for the next time you want to use them.
  6. Next fill in the "Student Name", "Access ID", and "Access Password" fields as shown for the student listed in this, as well as for any other students sent to you in separate email messages. IMPORTANT: Access IDs and Passwords are case sensitive. For each student, select your relationship to the student in the "Relationship" drop-down menu.

  1. When all the information is complete, click the "enter" button to create the account. You will only need one account per parent email address to access all of your Kentfield students.

If you already have a Parent Access Account, you can add a student to the account by following these steps:

  1. Log in to the account at https://ksd.powerschool.com (the PowerSchool Parent Portal). Enter your Parent Access Account User Name and Password (not the Student Access ID and Password), and click the "Submit" button.
  2. Click the "Account Preferences" icon.
  3. Click the "Students" tab.
  4. In the "My Students" area, click the "Add +" button.
  5. Enter the Student Name, Access ID, Access Password (using the information listed above), and choose your Realationship to the student. Then click the "Submit" button.
