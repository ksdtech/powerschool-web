To the parents of ~(first_name) ~(last_name):

Below you'll find a web address, and instructions for creating a Parent Access Account that will allow you to connect to the Kentfield School District's online registration system. After you create your parent account and log in, please click the "Registration" icon and fill out all pages of the online regstration form as soon as possible, so that we can schedule your child's classes and provide you with additional information.

To create a parent account, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to http://ps.kentfieldschools.org (the PowerSchool Parent Portal).
  2. Click the "Create Account" button at the bottom of the log in page.
  3. On the Create Account page, type in your (the parent's) First Name, Last Name and Email address in the appropriate fields.
  4. Choose a simple user name (suggestion: concatenate the initial of your first name and your entire last name).
  5. Choose a password that is at least 6 characters long, and type it into the "Password" and "Re-enter Password" fields.
  6. Next fill in the "Name", "Access ID", and "Access Password" fields as shown for each of your students listed below. For each student, select your relationship to the student in the "Relationship" drop-down menu.
  7. When all the information is complete, click the "enter" button to create the account.
    ~[tlist_sql; SELECT st.First_Name, st.Last_Name, st.Web_ID, st.Web_Password FROM Students st LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomText hid ON (hid.FieldNo=337 AND hid.KeyNo=st.DCID) WHERE hid.Value='~(home_id)' OR st.Student_Number=~(student_number)]
