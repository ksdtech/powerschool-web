Detailed Instructions
Validation: On each page, if you do not fill in required fields, or do not format the fields as required, red prompt(s) will appear in front of the required field(s). Your data will not be saved until all errors on the page have been corrected, and the Submit button has been clicked again.

Non-Returning Students: If the student is not returning to Kent or Bacich, you must fill out the exit information on Page 2 and click the Submit button at the bottom of that page.

New or Returning Students: If the student will be attending a District school, you must fill in or review the information on all pages (Pages 2-14) of the registration form, and click the Submit button at the bottom of each page. Sign and date the acknowledgement on Page 14, and click the Submit button on that page to complete the process.

Navigation: After each required page of the form has been submitted and your answers for the page have been validated, you may choose to return to this page or continue to the next page. When you return to this page, a check mark will appear in this list above in the box next to the name of the page to indicate that the page you submitted was successfully completed. You may also return to this page at any point by clicking the Registration icon in the navigation menu in the left sidebar.
