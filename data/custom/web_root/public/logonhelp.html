Problems logging on to the system?

Please contact your school directly if you have questions or comments about logon issues, progress reports, or other issues specific to your school or account.

For Kent School, contact Assistant Principal Jenny Walsh at jwalsh@kentfieldschools.org, or by phone at 415-458-5970.

For Bacich School, contact Assistant Principal Holly Edde at hedde@kentfieldschools.org, or by phone at 415-925-2220.

Please note that for privacy reasons, passwords will not be given out over the phone, and that to receive a password via email, your email address will have to be verified against school registration information.

Your school controls all access rights and site features for its PowerSchool system. For security purposes, we at PowerSchool have no knowledge of your individual logon passwords or your school configuration. We are therefore unable to assist you with logon, password, or other accessibility issues. For these issues, please contact your school directly. Thank you in advance for your understanding.
