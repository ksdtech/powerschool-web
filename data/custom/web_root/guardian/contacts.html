  Update Your Contact and Medical Information

Instructions
From this page, you can quickly access the parts of your student's registration forms that contain contact and medical information. Please verify or update the information on each of these pages whenever there is a change of your physical address, mailing address, parents' or emergency contacts' names, phone numbers, email addresses or information about the medical needs of your student.

After making any changes to a page, make sure to click the Submit button at the bottom of the page.

Please note: The phone number information you provide on the Contact Information page(s) will be used in the event of an emergency. An automated dialing system will call up to 6 phone numbers for each residence, taken from the "Home Phone", "Work Phone" and "Cell Phone" fields. Each of these numbers will be called until either live person or a voice mail system answers. Phone numbers with extensions will not be called.

You may return to this page at any point by clicking the Contacts icon in the icon banner above.
