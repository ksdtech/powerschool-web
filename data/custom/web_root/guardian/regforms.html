  Online Registration Forms

Welcome
Welcome to the Kentfield School District online registration and contact information system. Registration for the 2008-2009 school year begins in May, 2008. Please complete all information no later than Tuesday, May 20th.

Information you provide will be used by the school in many ways. For example:
  • Enrollment information will be used to determine the number of teachers needed for next year and to facilitate classroom placements.
  • Emergency contact information determines who is authorized to pick up your children in case of illness or emergency.
  • Medical information is used by the school nurse to insure your child receives proper health care.
Instructions
Click on the blue links below to go to each of the nine pages of the registration form. Please make sure you complete all pages. As you complete a page, remember to click the Submit button at the bottom of the page. After each page of the form is submitted, the system will bring you here and a check mark will appear in the box next to the name of the page to indicate that it has been completed.

You may return to this page at any point by clicking the Class Registration icon in the icon banner above.

Note: Only Page 1 is required for students who are not returning to Kent or Bacich. Pages 1 through 9 must be completed for new or returning students.
 
Required Documents for Current Students
The complete list of required documents for students returning to Bacich or Kent is as follows:
  • All pages (links above) of the Online District Registration Form completely filled out and updated where necessary
  • Signed Proof of Residency Affadavit and documentation (mailed to parents of returning students in the summer; see Page 3 for more information)
  • Authorization to Administer Medication and 72-Hour Medication Forms, if applicable (must be signed by a physician; see Page 8 for more information)
 
Required Documents for New Students
In addition to the items listed in Required Documents for Current Students, above, parents of new students entering the District must also provide these items to the school office:
  • Copy of student's birth certificate (or passport or baptismal certificate)
  • Immunization record ("Health Exam for School Entry"), signed by a physician. This form may be downloaded from the link above, or picked up at the school office.
  • Request for Records from Previous School (available from the school office)
  • All other information you wish the school to know about your child
Present these documents to the Bacich or Kent School offices during business hours to obtain a username and password. If the school offices are closed for vacation, you may go to the District office in Kentfield. If you cannot appear in person because you currently live outside of the area, or cannot provide the required documents, please speak with
  • Bacich School Secretary, at (415) 925-2220,
  • Susan Merrill, the Kent Administrative Assistant, at (415) 458-5974, or
  • Sylvia Pesenti, District Superintendent's Assistant, at (415) 925-2230.
For Students entering Kent School: You will need to schedule Math and Language Arts placement tests with the Kent school office (see phone number above) as soon as possible so that your student's schedule can be created.
