Registration Forms
Parents must fill out all applicable forms below, as well as provide required paper documents to the school office, for registration to be complete. The Additional Residence Information form is only for those students who live with parents at a second residence in addition to the residence that is within District boundaries.

Remember to click the Submit button at the bottom of each form to record your changes or just to accept the data as it is. After you have visited and submitted each form page, you will be returned to this page, and the box corresponding to the form will be checked.
 Page 1 - Name and Birth Information
 Page 2 - Qualifying Residence Information
 Page 3 - Additional Residence Information
 Page 4 - Previous School, Special Programs and Demographics
 Page 5 - Home Language Survey
 Page 6 - Emergency Contact Information
 Page 7 - Medical Providers, Insurance and Health Information
 Page 8 - Agreements
