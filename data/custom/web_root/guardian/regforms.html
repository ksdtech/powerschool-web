Online Registration Forms

Important Note: Registration Is Open for Kindergarten Only
Registration for the 2012-2013 school year is now open only for students who will be entering Kindergarten. If you have students who will be returning to a Kentfield School District school next fall, or who will be entering a District school in any grade other than Kindergarten, please STOP now. The registration window for 2012-2013 Grades 1-8 will open in late March.
Welcome
Welcome to the Kentfield School District online registration and contact information system. The confidential information you provide will be used by the school in many ways. For example:
  • Enrollment information will be used to determine the number of teachers needed for next year and to facilitate classroom placements.
  • Contact information determines which phone numbers will be called in the event of an emergency.
  • Authorized adults are those whom you authorize to pick up your children in case of illness or emergency.
  • Medical information is used by the school nurse to insure your child receives proper health care.
Required Documents for Current Students
The complete list of required documents for students returning to Bacich or Kent is as follows:
  1. All pages (links above) of the Online District Registration Form completely filled out and updated where necessary
  2. Signed Proof of Residency Affadavit and documentation, for incoming 5th grade students (see Page 3 for more information)
  3. Proof of Immunization (including Tdap booster for incoming 7th and 8th grade students)
  4. Authorization to Administer Medication and 72-Hour Medication Forms, if applicable (must be signed by a physician; see Page 8 for more information)
Required Documents for New Students
Parents of new students entering the District must provide these items to the school office:
  1. All pages (links above) of the Online District Registration Form completely filled out and updated where necessary
  2. Copy of student's birth certificate (or passport or baptismal certificate)
  3. Signed Proof of Residency Affadavit and documentation, if required (see Page 3 for more information)
  4. ("Health Exam for School Entry"), signed by a physician. This form may be downloaded from the link above, or picked up at the school office.
  5. Proof of Immunization (including Tdap booster for incoming 7th and 8th grade students)
  6. Authorization to Administer Medication and 72-Hour Medication Forms, if applicable (must be signed by a physician; see Page 8 for more information)
  7. Request for Records from Previous School (available from the school office)
  8. All other information you wish the school to know about your child
Present these documents to the Bacich or Kent School offices during business hours. If the school offices are closed for vacation, you may go to the District office, located at 750 College Avenue in Kentfield. If you cannot appear in person because you currently live outside of the area, or cannot provide the required documents, please speak with
  • Bacich School Office, at (415) 925-2220,
  • Kent School Office, at (415) 458-5970, or
  • District Office, at (415) 458-5130.
For Students entering Kent School: In order to create the class schedule for new students at Kent School, parents must schedule Math and Language Arts placement tests with the Kent School Office (see phone number above) as soon as possible.
Instructions
Click on the blue links below to go to each of the pages of the registration form. Please make sure you complete all pages. As you complete a page, remember to click the Submit button at the bottom of the page.
If you do not fill in required fields, or do not format the fields as required, red prompt(s) will appear in front of the required field(s). Your data will not be saved until all errors on the page have been corrected, and the Submit button has been clicked again.
After each page of the form is submitted, the system will bring you here and a check mark will appear in the box next to the name of the page to indicate that it has been completed.
Please note: The phone number information you provide on the Residence Information page(s) will be used in the event of an emergency. An automated dialing system will call up to 6 phone numbers for each residence, taken from the "Home Phone", "Work Phone" and "Cell Phone" fields. Each of these numbers will be called until either a person or a voice mail system answers. Phone numbers with extensions will not be called.
You may return to this page at any point by clicking the Registration icon in the icon banner above.
