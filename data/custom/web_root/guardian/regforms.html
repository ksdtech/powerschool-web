  Online Registration Forms

Registration Requirements and Procedures
Registration for the school year that begins in August 2008 will open on May 9, 2008. This year, registration forms (including the Home Language Survey, the Emergency Contact Form, music class selection for grades 5 through 8 and the eighth grade choice of enrichment classes) will be filled out online. Please read the sections below for additional requirements and details for registering current and new students.
 
Online Registration Forms
Parents must fill out all applicable forms below, as well as provide required paper documents to the school office, for registration to be complete.

If your student will not be enrolling at either Bacich or Kent in the fall of 2008, you must fill out Page 1, the Enrollment Information Form only.

Page 3, the Additional Residence Information Form is only for those students who live with parents at a second residence in addition to the residence that is within District boundaries.

Remember to click the Submit button at the bottom of each form to record your changes or just to accept the data as it is. After you have visited and submitted each form page, you will be returned to this page, and the box corresponding to the form will be checked.
 
Page 1 - Enrollment Information
Page 2 - Name, Birth and Custody Information
Page 3 - Qualifying Residence Information
Page 4 - Additional Residence Information
Page 5 - Emergency Contact Information
Page 6 - Previous School and Demographics
Page 7 - Home Language Survey
Page 8 - Medical Providers, Insurance and Health Information
Page 9 - Agreements
 
Registration Procedure for Current Students
Parents of current students will receive instructions by email on how to access the online registration system with their user name and password. If you have not received or have lost these instructions, please call the school office for a replacement copy. The deadline for completing the online registration forms for current students is May 16 for students in the current fourth through seventh grades (entering fifth through eighth grades in August 2008).
 
Required Documents for Current Students
Please note that the District is asking families of returning students to re-submit a proof of current residency. This proof must be submitted to the District Office prior to August 1.

The complete list of required documents for students returning to Bacich or Kent is as follows:
  • Proof of Residence documents (minimum of 3 pieces required):
    • Copy of Property Tax Bill or copy of Rental/Lease/Purchase Agreeement
    • Copy of most recent PG&E bill (page 3 indicating service address) or letter of initiation of service
    • Copy of most recent Marin Municipal Water District bill or letter of initiation of service
  • Immunization Record (Health Examination for School Entry). Hepatitis B and 2 MMR immunizations must be completed, or an immunization series started, prior to entry in the seventh grade.
  • Online District Registration Form (which includes the Home Language Survey), with revisions to prior data made where necessary
  • Online Medical Information and Emergency Contact Forms, with revisions to prior data made where necessary
 
Registration Procedure for New Students
In order to obtain user names and passwords for the online registration system, parents of students new to the Kentfield School District must bring required documents to the Bacich or Kent School offices. If you cannot appear in person because you currently live outside of the area, or cannot provide the required documents, please speak with Jan Canon, the School Secretary at Bacich, at (415) 925-2220, or with Susan Merrill, the Administrative Assistant at Kent, at (415) 458-5974, to see if special arrangements can be made.
 
Required Documents for New Students
Please review this list of requirements, in order to ensure that all documentation for your child's entry into the District is complete. No child will be admitted until ALL of the items listed below are on file in the school office. With the exception of the Health Examination for School Entry and the Math Placement Test, all forms must be returned to the school office by August 1. If the office is closed, please bring forms to the District Office at 790 College Avenue in Kentfield.
  • All items listed in Required Documents for Current Students, above
  • Copy of Birth Certificate (or passport or baptismal certificate)
  • Online Request for Records from Previous School
  • Online Parent Permission for Field Trips
  • All other information you wish the school to know about your child
Math Placement Test for New Students at Kent Middle School
Parents of new students who will be entering grades 5 through 8 must schedule a Math Placement Test, to be administered by a school employee, as soon as possible. Math placement will determined by a math teacher.
