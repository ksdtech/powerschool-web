  Online Registration Forms

Welcome to the Kentfield School District online registration and contact information system. Registration for the 2008-2009 school year begins on May 9, 2008. Please complete all information no later than Tuesday, May 27th.

Information you provide will be used by the school in many ways. For example:
  • Intent to Return information will be used to determine the number of teachers needed for next year and to facilitate classroom placements.
  • Emergency contact information determines who is authorized to pick up your children in case of illness or emergency.
  • Medical information is used by the school nurse to insure your child is cared for.
Please make sure you complete all sections. As you complete a section, remember to chick the Submit button. After you click Submit the system will return you to the main page. A check mark will appear in the box to the left of the section once it is complete.

Registration Procedure for Current Students
Parents of current students will receive instructions by email on how to access the online registration system with their user name and password. If you have not received or have lost these instructions, please call the school office for a replacement copy. The deadline for completing the online registration forms for current students is May 16 for students in the current fourth through seventh grades (entering fifth through eighth grades in August 2008).
 
Required Documents for Current Students
The complete list of required documents for students returning to Bacich or Kent is as follows:
  • Proof of Residence documents (see Page 3)
  • 7th Grade Only: Immunization Record (Health Examination for School Entry) (see Page 8)
  • Online District Registration Form (which includes the Home Language Survey), with revisions to prior data made where necessary
  • Online Medical Information and Emergency Contact Forms, with revisions to prior data made where necessary
 
Registration Procedure for New Students
Required Documents for New Students
In addition to the items listed in Required Documents for Current Students, above, students entering the District must also provide these items to the school office:
  • Copy of Birth Certificate (or passport or baptismal certificate)
  • Online Request for Records from Previous School
  • Online Parent Permission for Field Trips
  • All other information you wish the school to know about your child
Present these documents to the Bacich or Kent School offices during business hours to obtain a username and password. If the school offices are closed for vacation, you may deliver them to the District office at 790 College Avenue, Kentfield. If you cannot appear in person because you currently live outside of the area, or cannot provide the required documents, please speak with
Jan Canon, the Bacich School Secretary, at (415) 925-2220,
Susan Merrill, the Kent Administrative Assistant, at (415) 458-5974, or
Sylvia Pesenti, District Superintendent's Assistant, at (415) 925-2230.
For Students entering Kent School: You will need to schedule a short Math Placement test for your child with the Kent school office as soon as possible.
