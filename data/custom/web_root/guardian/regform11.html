Kentfield Student Travel Program

The Kentfield School District encourages all families to actively participate in the Student Travel Program (STP), a neighborhood-focused initiative to advocate green travel alternatives, as well as to address traffic congestion and safety at our schools. The goal of the program is to offer students safer and greener alternatives to get to and from school by connecting parents with resources such as suggested safe route maps, schedules for walking and cycling, and carpooling options all customized by neighborhood.

Know Your Neighborhood Suggested Route Map to Bacich and Kent

The Kentfield Student Travel Program organizes our district by neighborhoods. Each neighborhood has a suggested route map to Bacich and Kent, and a volunteer parent Neighborhood Captain who will assist you in finding other parents to walk, bike, or carpool together if desired. The Captains also advocate for safety concerns on behalf of the families in their neighborhood. We will be relying on the neighborhood you select on this page to determine your neighborhood.
Electing to participate in this program will ensure that you receive the suggested route map for your neighborhood and alternatives to walk, bike, or carpool to school. It does not obligate you to do anything other than be informed of the Student Travel Program options available in your neighborhood.
