KIK Directory Form 2013-2014

11. KIK Directory Form 2013-2014
Important! THIS IS THE ONLY WAY TO BE LISTED IN THE 2013-2014 DIRECTORY
You MUST fill out the information below.
* If you do NOT want to be listed in the directory, you must select the "Do Not List" choice below.
* If you are entering information for a student attending the school district for the first time (2013-2014 school year), you must choose the "Replace" option and completely fill in the form below.
* Please only list the children who will attend Bacich or Kent schools.
* If you have multiple students you are registering, you can only enter the information for one student, and then you MUST choose the "Another Student" option for the other students in your family.
If you have problems filling out the information on this page, please contact the school offices for assistance.
