Safe Routes to Schools
The Kentfield School District is excited to launch a new program to address traffic congestion and safety at our schools.  Our goal is to offer parents safer and greener alternatives to get to and from school by connecting you with resources such as safe routes maps, schedules for walking and cycling school buses, and carpooling options customized by neighborhood.
Travel Plan
The new Safe Routes to Schools Travel Plan will organize our district by neighborhoods.  Each neighborhood will receive suggested route maps and have assigned at least one parent captain who will assist you in finding other parents to walk, bike, or carpool together. We will be relying on the neighborhood you select on this page to determine your neigborhood.
