3a. Qualifying Residence Information
Please note that the District requires all families to submit proof of residency at an address that is within District boundaries. Proof must be provided to the District Office each year, prior to August 1st.

Proof of Residency documents (1 of each document is required):
  • Copy of Property Tax Bill or copy of Rental/Lease/Purchase Agreeement
  • Copy of most recent PG&E bill (page 3 indicating service address) or letter of initiation of service
  • Copy of most recent Marin Municipal Water District bill or letter of initiation of service
Notes: In case of emergency, students may only be released to parents, legal guardians, or emergency contacts (listed on Page 5) designated by a parent or legal guardian. If some of the student's parents or legal guardians live at another address, use the Additional Residence form (Page 4) to list their names and contact information. This page is only for information about those parents who live at the residence that qualifies the student for enrollment in the District.

Legal Guardianship is legally defined in Section 20 of the Family Law Act; Part 5 of the Child Welfare Act; Part 1, Division 5 of the Child, Youth and Family Enhancement Act; or Section 23 of the Family Law Act. Legal Guardianship may also be established by a temporary or permanent guardianship order under the Child Welfare Act, or by way of a court order or agreement in accordance with the Family Law Act.
