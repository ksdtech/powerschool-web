5a. Qualifying Residence Information
Please note that the District requires all families to submit proof of residency each year a child is attending a district school. Proof must be provided to the District Office prior to August 1st.

Proof of Residency documents (1 of each document is required):
  • Copy of Property Tax Bill or copy of Rental/Lease/Purchase Agreeement
  • Copy of most recent PG&E bill (page 3 indicating service address) or letter of initiation of service
  • Copy of most recent Marin Municipal Water District bill or letter of initiation of service
