General Instructions
Visit any registration pages that do not have the green checkmark next to them, make corrections on those pages, and click the Submit button. Please note that pages with required fields will not allow submission unless those fields are properly entered. As you complete and successfully submit each page, the green checkmark will appear to the left of each page name.

When all pages are checked off, fill out the Electronic Signature Acknowledgement section below and click the Submit button.
