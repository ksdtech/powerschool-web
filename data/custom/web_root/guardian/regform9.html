9b. Release of Pupil Directory Information
The District may release Pupil Directory Information to non-profit organizations and the news media in accordance with California law. As explained in the Annual Notice, parents of students aged 17 or younger have the right to withhold the release of this information. If you do not wish directory information about your student released (page 1), please check the box indicated below.
Note that this will prohibit the district from providing the student's name and other information to the news media, interested schools, parent-teacher associations, interested employers, and similar parties.

9e. Notification of Pesticide Application
The Healthy Schools Act of 2000 requires that all schools provide parents or guardians annual written notice of expected pesticide use on school sites. While the District does not plan to use pesticides, parents or guardians may request prior notification of individual pesticide applications at school sites. Please click the link below to download or view more information about your rights concerning notification. Then check the appropriate box below if you wish to receive email notifications of pesticide applications.
   Integrated Pest Management Notice
   YES, I (we) WOULD LIKE TO BE NOTIFIED before each pesticide application at this school
   No, I (we) DO NOT WISH TO BE NOTIFIED before each pesticide application at this school
9f. Walking Off-Campus Permission Form
During the school year the District may conduct emergency evaluation drills which require students and teachers to walk to a local off-campus staging area near the school site. Also, teachers may take their classes on walking field trips in the school community. The purpose of such walks is for the students to visit neighborhood sites and educational places in our community such as local parks, the Kentfield Post Office, Woodlands Market, and the fire department. Teachers will always notify parents when any off-campus field trips take place. Separate permission forms will always be used when any form of transportation is involved. Please click the link below for more information, then check the box to grant permission for your child to participate in these activities.
   Off-Campus Permission Form
   YES, I (we) GIVE PERMISSION for my child to participate in supervised neighborhood walks during the 2012-2013 school year
   No, I (we) DO NOT GIVE PERMISSION for my child to participate in supervised neighborhood walks during the 2012-2013 school year

Please click the link to download and read this agreement with your student and then check the box to indicate your agreement and approval.

9i. Technology - Google Apps
The Kentfield School District uses Google Apps for Education as a fundamental platform for student learning. Students create and share work and communicate with teachers and each other using Google Apps. According to Google’s Terms of Service, any user of Google Apps under the age of 13 must obtain the consent of his or her parents. Please click the links below to obtain more information and the then check the box to give your permission for the District to set up a Google Apps for Education account for this student.
If you change your approval or disapproval of a Google Apps account during the school year, please submit your change in writing to the school office so that the student's Google Apps account can be enabled or disabled.

9j. Technology - Other Social Learning Applications
The Kentfield School District also uses other "social learning" web applications such as Edmodo.com, VoiceThread.com, to foster important communication and collaboration between students, teachers and each other as an essential part of the learning environment. These applications are designed for use by schools, and all use in school is moderated by District staff to protect the privacy of our students. For the district to be able to establish student accounts in these services, parents must grant permission for under-age students (under the age of 18 or 13). Please click the links below to review the terms and conditions for these services and then check the box to give your permission for the District to maintain accounts for your students as necessary.
