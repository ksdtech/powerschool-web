Family and kik School Directory Information
In order for the District to be able to contact parents, it is essential that you correct and complete all the appropriate fields on this page. The information on this page will also be used as the source for the kik School Directory.
It is important that you correctly check or uncheck the fields that control whether your names, home addresses, phones and email addresses will be printed in, or omitted from, the kik School Directory. This is the only data that will be used to create the kik School Directory.
After you have entered the data, carefully verify that the preview for your family shown at the bottom of the page is correct and mark it as approved, then make sure to press the Submit button.
If you do not check the approval box at the bottom of this page, your family WILL NOT be included in kik School Directory.

VERY IMPORTANT: If you do not want any family information listed in the kik School Directory, this is the only place to opt-out completely. To omit specific fields (names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, etc.) you must place a check mark in the appropriate "Exclude" boxes in the above sections.
