Immunizations
Note for students entering 7th Grade only: Submit an immunization record ("Health Exam for School Entry") to the school office, signed by a physician that documents that one HepatitisB and two MMR immunizations have been completed, or that these immunization series have been started prior to entering 7th grade. 11. Medications - Daily and 72 hour Supply
In order to administer medication, the District requires a medication form, signed by a doctor. The forms for daily medication ("Authorization to Administer Medication") and for 72 hour emergency medication ("72 Hour Medication Form for Emergency Preparedness") are available in the school office, or can be downloaded from the Marin County Office of Education Medications page in English or Spanish versions.

Please complete the appropriate form(s) and return them to the school office. Then bring the required medication in the original, currently dated prescription container labeled with the student's name on the first day of school.
