5. Authorized Adults to Pick up Your Child in Case of Emergency
The contact information you provide on this page determines who is authorized to pick up your children in case of illness, injury, or emergency. No student will be released to anyone other than the parents, guardians, or the adults listed below. Please make sure that you have an emergency family plan and that your contacts are aware of it. It is extremely important that these questions be answered accurately and that the contact information be kept current.

List the names of up to three relatives, neighbors, or friends in close proximity to the school to whom the school may release your child or contact if the student's parents or guardians cannot be reached.

In selecting someone to whom you authorize the release of your child, consider:
  1. Would your child feel safe and comfortable with this person or family?
  2. Could this person care for your child for several days?
  3. Is this person prepared to handle any special medical needs your child requires?
You may enter the first names of two people as a single contact only if the persons share the same last name and phone numbers; otherwise, enter the names and phone numbers as separate contacts.
