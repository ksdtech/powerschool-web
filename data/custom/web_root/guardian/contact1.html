Qualifying Residence Emergency Dialing Information
The information you provide on this page will be used in the event of an emergency. An automated dialing system will call up to 3 phone numbers continually, until a live person is reached.
Instructions: Please select three different contact phone numbers for use by the District emergency dialing system. These must be direct, daytime phone numbers. Please do not select phone numbers that connect to automated answering machines or receptionists. If the number you want to use does not appear in the available phone number menus, you must go back to the Qualifying Residence Information page of your registration information and add the phone number there first, then return to this page.
