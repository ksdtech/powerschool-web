Student Information
Use the navigation pane on the left to work with the students listed.
  • Click on the student's last name to go to your default student screen.
  • Click on the student's first name to go to the last student screen viewed.
    i.e. - If you last looked at an Attendance page, clicking the student's first name will take you to the Attendance page for that student.
  • Click on a class name to change the current list of students to those in that class.
Parent Email Addresses for Section ID# ~[gpv:sectionid]
Instructions: To download these email contacts in a CSV file for use with GMail, click This Link to popup a new window
that contains a text field with the contact data.
Now launch a text application, either TextEdit on a Mac, or Notepad on a Windows PC.
Then select the entire contents of the text field and copy it to the text application. Save the file.
After saving the file, you may import it into GMail's Contacts.
~[tlist_sql; SELECT mf.Value AS First, st.Mother AS Last, 'Mother of '||st.First_Name||' '||st.Last_Name as Notes, me.Value As Email, st.Home_Phone As Phone, st.Last_Name, st.First_Name FROM Students st JOIN cc ON (cc.StudentID=st.ID AND cc.SectionID=~[gpv:sectionid]) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars mf ON (mf.FieldNo=223 AND mf.KeyNo=st.DCID) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars me ON (me.FieldNo=405 AND me.KeyNo=st.DCID) WHERE me.Value IS NOT NULL UNION ALL SELECT ff.Value AS First, st.Father AS Last, 'Father of '||st.First_Name||' '||st.Last_Name as Notes, fe.Value As Email, st.Home_Phone As Phone, st.Last_Name, st.First_Name FROM Students st JOIN cc ON (cc.StudentID=st.ID AND cc.SectionID=~[gpv:sectionid]) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars ff ON (ff.FieldNo=222 AND ff.KeyNo=st.DCID) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars fe ON (fe.FieldNo=404 AND fe.KeyNo=st.DCID) WHERE fe.Value IS NOT NULL UNION ALL SELECT m2f.Value AS First, m2l.Value as Last, 'NC Mother of '||st.First_Name||' '||st.Last_Name as Notes, m2e.Value As Email, '' As Phone, st.Last_Name, st.First_Name FROM Students st JOIN cc ON (cc.StudentID=st.ID AND cc.SectionID=~[gpv:sectionid]) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars m2l ON (m2l.FieldNo=414 AND m2l.KeyNo=st.DCID) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars m2f ON (m2f.FieldNo=415 AND m2f.KeyNo=st.DCID) LEFT JOIN CustomVarchars m2e ON (m2e.FieldNo=138 AND m2e.KeyNo=st.DCID) WHERE m2e.Value IS NOT NULL UNION ALL SELECT f2f.Value AS First, f2l.Value as Last, 'NC Father of '||st.First_Name||' '||st.Last_Name as Notes, f2e.Value As Email, '' As Phone, st.Last_Name, st.First_Name FROM Students st JOIN cc ON (cc.StudentID=st.ID AND cc.SectionID=~[gpv:sectionid]) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars f2l ON (f2l.FieldNo=418 AND f2l.KeyNo=st.DCID) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars f2f ON (f2f.FieldNo=419 AND f2f.KeyNo=st.DCID) LEFT OUTER JOIN CustomVarchars f2e ON (f2e.FieldNo=153 AND f2e.KeyNo=st.DCID) WHERE f2e.Value IS NOT NULL ORDER BY 6, 7, 3 ;alternatecolor] [/tlist_sql]
